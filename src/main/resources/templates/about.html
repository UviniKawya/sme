About This Platform

The SME Digital Readiness Assessment Platform helps small and medium enterprises evaluate their digital maturity across five key dimensions: Infrastructure, Finance, Skills, Cybersecurity, and Strategy.

Features

  • Comprehensive 25-question assessment with Likert-scale scoring
  • Real-time progress tracking and scoring
  • Inventory management with low-stock alerts
  • Sales tracking with payment status monitoring
  • Interactive dashboard with visualizations
  • SME registration and profile management

Technology

Spring Boot 3 Java 17 Thymeleaf H2 Database JPA/Hibernate HTML/CSS/JavaScript
